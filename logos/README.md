# Sponsor Logos

This directory is for storing sponsor logo images that will be displayed in the footer of the website.

## How to Add Logos

1. Upload your logo files to this directory
2. Name them according to the organization:
   - `epfl-logo.png` - EPFL Swiss Plasma Center logo
   - `icpc-logo.png` - Piero Caldirola International Centre logo
   - `cnr-logo.png` - CNR (Istituto per la Scienza e la Tecnologia dei Plasmi) logo
   - `milano-bicocca-logo.png` - Universit√† degli Studi di Milano-Bicocca logo

## Logo Requirements

- **Format**: PNG, SVG, or JPG
- **Size**: Recommended max width 300px, max height 100px
- **Background**: Transparent or white background works best
- **Colors**: Logos will be displayed with white/light colors on dark background

## Notes

- The website will automatically display the logos once they are uploaded
- Placeholder text will be hidden once the images are added
- Logos are automatically filtered to white/light colors to match the dark footer
- Responsive design ensures logos look good on all devices
