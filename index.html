About the Workshop

The Varenna–Lausanne Workshop is a prestigious biennial joint workshop that brings together leading researchers, academics, and practitioners from around the world. This workshop series fosters collaboration and knowledge exchange between the research communities of Varenna and Lausanne, two centers of academic excellence.

Our workshops provide a unique platform for discussing cutting-edge research, sharing innovative ideas, and building lasting international collaborations. Participants have the opportunity to engage in in-depth discussions, attend keynote presentations, and network with peers in an intimate and intellectually stimulating environment.

About the Locations

Varenna, Italy: A picturesque town on the eastern shore of Lake Como, Varenna provides a serene and inspiring setting for academic exchange. The town is renowned for its natural beauty and historic charm.

Lausanne, Switzerland: Home to world-class research institutions, Lausanne sits on the shores of Lake Geneva and offers a vibrant international atmosphere with excellent conference facilities.

Program

The workshop program typically includes:

  • Keynote Lectures - Presentations by distinguished speakers on current research topics
  • Contributed Talks - Presentations by participants on their latest research
  • Poster Sessions - Interactive displays of ongoing research projects
  • Discussion Panels - Collaborative sessions on emerging challenges and opportunities
  • Networking Events - Social activities to foster collaboration and exchange

Note: The detailed program for the next workshop will be published closer to the event date.
